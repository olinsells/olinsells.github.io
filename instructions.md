# About Page:

## What?

This is a project for the class Products & Markets at Olin College of Engineering. It is a MVP (minimum viable product) for a platform that lets Students list items they are selling in one centralized location. We plan to operate this website until at least the end of March 2026\. We do not handle payment or logistics, it’s primarily just a place to show things students are selling\!

Anyone can post listings or contact others about their listing. Please keep the listings real for the time being cause we want to pass this project 👉👈

## How? (to make a listing)

Click on the “Post an item” button in the top right of the webpage. It will take you to a Google form that you can fill out. The form asks for a product name, description, price, seller name, contact email, and an image. The item should appear on the website within a minute of submitting the form.

Instructions to remove a listing can be found by clicking the “Remove listing” button or going to this page \<LINK REMOVE A LISTING PAGE\>

To edit a listing it is best to just delete your old listing and make a new one.

## Why?

We are trying to see if an “Olin Depop” platform would be useful so we are running this experiment to test it.
