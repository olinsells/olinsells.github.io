# Removing a Listing

To remove a listing please go to the Listings Spreadsheet \<SPREADSHEET LINK\> and delete the row (horizontal) associated with your listing. 

The easiest way to delete an entire row on Google sheets is to:  
\-Left click on the number on the far left of your screen associated with your listing (the row should now be highlighted blue)  
\-Now right click the same number and click the option “Delete Row”
